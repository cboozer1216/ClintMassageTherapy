About Me

Hello! I'm Clint Boozer. I am a dedicated massage therapist based in Spokane, WA. My journey into bodywork is driven by a passion for helping others achieve physical relief and mental relaxation.

I combine a strong foundation in anatomy with intuitive touch to create personalized sessions for every client. Whether you are an athlete looking to recover, or simply seeking relief from the stress of daily life, my goal is to facilitate healing and wellness.

In addition to my practice, I am deeply interested in holistic wellness and the science of recovery. I look forward to working with you to create a treatment plan that fits your specific needs.
